About NIC Validation
  • Old NIC: 9 digits + V/X (e.g., 716941485V)
  • New NIC: 12 digits (e.g., 197169401485)
  • Gender: Day value 1-366 = Male, 501-866 = Female
  • Birth Year: Extracted from first 2 or 4 digits
  • Date Format: DD-MM-YYYY (e.g., 25-12-1990)
